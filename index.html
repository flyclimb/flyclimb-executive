Frequently Asked Questions

How long does processing take?

Most document sets are processed in 5-15 minutes. You'll receive an email as soon as your briefing is ready.

What are the file size and quantity limits?

You can upload up to 5 files per conversation, with each file limited to 2MB. We support Excel (.xlsx, .xls), PDF, and images (JPG, PNG).

How long do you keep my data?

We retain your data for 24 hours only. After that, all uploaded documents and generated narratives are permanently deleted from our systems. If you need to continue working with the same documents, simply re-upload them.

What makes FlyClimb Executive different from ChatGPT?

FlyClimb Executive is purpose-built for executives. We process multiple document types together, deliver via email asynchronously, and generate presentation-specific talking points - not generic summaries.

Can I customize the output format?

Yes! You can specify your presentation length, audience type, and focus area. We'll tailor the narrative accordingly.

Is my data secure during processing?

Absolutely. All processing happens on secure AWS infrastructure with encryption. We never share your data with third parties, and everything is automatically deleted after 24 hours.
